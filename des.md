Title: Client Portal and Billing SaaS for UK Accounting Firms
categroy: SaaS platforms
location country: uk
description: A SaaS platform built for UK accounting firms to manage client communication, billing workflows, and financial document exchange in a single system. Designed to reduce manual coordination and improve billing accuracy across client portfolios.
detailed content: UK-based accounting firms were managing client communication, invoicing, and document sharing across disconnected tools, leading to inefficiencies and missed billing opportunities. CodexaSoft developed a centralized SaaS platform that combines secure client portals with integrated billing workflows. The system supports multi-client environments with strict access controls and handles recurring billing cycles across hundreds of client accounts. It enables firms to streamline operations while maintaining transparency and accountability in client interactions.
challenge: Fragmented tools caused inconsistent client communication and billing delays, recurring invoicing required flexible configuration across different client agreements, secure document sharing needed strict access controls and auditability, system had to handle concurrent usage across multiple firms and clients, compliance with UK data protection expectations required careful data handling
solution: A tenant-isolated architecture was implemented to ensure each accounting firm operates within a secure and independent environment. Django-based backend services were structured around billing and client management domains to support complex invoicing logic without coupling. PostgreSQL was used to manage transactional billing data with consistency guarantees. Background workers using Celery handled invoice generation and payment reconciliation asynchronously. The frontend was designed with Next.js to provide fast navigation and secure client access to financial documents.
results: reduced billing cycle time by 40 percent, improved invoice collection rate by 25 percent, supported over 500 active client accounts per firm, achieved consistent system response times under 250ms, decreased manual coordination efforts across teams by 50 percent
key features: secure client portal with role-based access, recurring and one-time invoice generation, automated payment tracking and reconciliation, document sharing with access-level restrictions, client activity audit logs, configurable billing cycles per client, email notifications for invoices and updates, multi-client dashboard for firm administrators
technologies: Next.js, Django, PostgreSQL, Redis, Celery, AWS
